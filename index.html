Andre Barros, Brazilian, 46 years old, holds an MBA from MIT Sloan School of Management and a LL.M from Stanford Law School. Mr. Barros is currently a CEO of SJK Capital and Genesis Mining, founder of HVC Par and a partner at ACCE Capital (a venture capital firm). Previously, Mr. Barros was a Director for Strategic Partnerships in South America for Flex, a Fortune 500 Company listed on NASDAQ (NASDAQ:FLEX) and had spent 5 years as Chief Financial Officer and Executive Director of Ferrous Resources Limited (Junior Mining Company formed in the UK with operations in Brazil). Before Ferrous Resources Limited, Mr. Barros was a Partner and Chief Financial Officer of FIR Capital Partners (a Brazilian Venture Capital Firm focused on early stage IT and Biotech investments) from 2001 to 2007. Mr. Barros began his career in 1993 at Arthur Andersen, which he left in 2001 as an audit manager.
